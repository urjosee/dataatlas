About Us

At Data Atlas we aim to build a dynamic and cumulative process that results in the production of knowledge. Inspired from the Collective Learning group at the MIT Media Labs, we have built a tool that will help you access complex datasets and learn from them. We develop large visualization engines and help transform data into stories. Reading data doesn't always have to be about boring and huge excel sheets and neither does it just have to be about looking at numbers. We aim to make learning fun for you by taking you on a journey with us through each dataset with colorful visualizations and interactive elements that you can try out yourself. We want you to focus on the learning whilst we constantly try to make the experience simpler and smoother for you. If you want us to add any topic of your intetest, do drop in a message at the Contact Us section below and we will try our best to get it done for you. Happy Learning!
